Information We Collect

Kab Ride collects necessary information to provide our ride-hailing services, including:

  • Location data for ride matching and navigation
  • Contact information for account creation
  • Payment information for fare processing
  • App usage data for service improvement
  • Driver verification documents for safety

How We Use Your Information

We use collected data solely for providing and improving our transportation services, including:

  • Matching riders with nearby drivers
  • Providing real-time navigation and ETAs
  • Processing secure payments
  • Ensuring safety and verifying drivers
  • Improving our service quality

Data Protection

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information and never share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.
